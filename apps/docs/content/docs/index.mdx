---
title: Welcome
description: mpckit is a hosted MPC signing layer. One bearer token, every chain ika supports, no private keys held on your servers or ours.
---

mpckit lets you sign on every chain ika supports from a single HTTP
API. The protocol runs as two-party MPC anchored on Sui mainnet; your
server never touches a full private key, and neither do we.
